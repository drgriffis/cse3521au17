Extra Credit: AI Seminar

Attending a seminar: AI in practice!

CSE has an ongoing seminar series on Artificial Intelligence, highlighting student research at OSU on AI methods and applications.

To put the things you're learning in perspective, you can earn extra credit (up to +3% on your grade) by attending one of these seminars and writing a report on what you learned.

Seminar schedule

Seminars occur on Friday afternoons, from 3-4pm, in Dreese 480.

The report

To get your extra credit, please write up a half-page to one-page report describing three things:

  1. Which seminar you attended
  2. What kind of problem the speaker was working on solving, and how it fits into the topics we're discussing in class. For example, what is the goal they're trying to achieve? What kind of models or states were being considered? How did they evaluate performance? How does this compare to, say, Watson playing Jeapordy?
  3. What you learned, and what questions you have. For example, what things did they try that worked, and what didn't? What kinds of information (or features) did they use for the task? What things did they talk about that made you say, "Huh?"

Send me your report as an email.
